Activities

This page describes the activities I participated in at MIT, which I'm glad to document here for my own memory's sake.

Club Involvement

StartLabs: Advisor (2019-2020), Co-Director (2018 Year), Vice President of Partner Relations (2017 Year). Worked on building out relationships between StartLabs and the Boston entrepreneurship community; hosted speaker/ideation events like SEED, Entrepalooza, and numerous smaller events; planned and executed Tech Treks to Israel and Switzerland

StartLabs Entrepreneurship Experience Day (SEED): Recruited and coordinated speakers, organized planning and day-of logistics

Entrepalooza 2017: Put together first iteration of premier undergrad fall entrepreneurship event. Recruited and coordinated speakers, organized back-end logistics, coordinated funding, organized marketing and the Bad Ideas Pitch Competition. See also Entrepalooza 2018, where I moderated the keynote fireside chat with Rich Miner.

Israel Tech Trek: Raised $25K for 12 students in two months to run a pilot international “Tech Trek” to Israel, organizing ground logistics and events for 9 days of travel. Similar work was put in to help with our 2018 Switzerland Tech Trek.

MacGregor House Committee (HouseComm): Served in various positions, as MacGregor President (2019 Year, 1), H Entry Chair (2016 – 2018, 2), Peer Mentor (2017 – Present, 3), and other miscellaneous positions.

1. Interfaced with administration to work on issues like dorm rooming selection, renovations, and quality of life changes. Lead weekly meetings of student house leadership; met weekly with student leaders in the Undergraduate Association and Dormitory Council.

2. Conceived and hosted social events, such as dinner trips, arts and crafts events, movie nights, and more for our tight-knit community of forty-four students; worked with the Heads of House and Area Director on our GRT search and relocation of Spanish House, among many other decisions

3. Worked with incoming freshmen to help in their transition to college life, hosting monthly study breaks for 13 (2018-2019) and 9 (2019-2020) first years, in addition to supporting dorm-wide events

Other:

Spokes Across America: Selected as part of eight person team to teach and bike across the continental U.S. in summer 2020. Working on finances, sponsorships, and website.

MIT Clean Energy Prize 2020: Director of Mentorship. Leading efforts to source mentors and match them with semifinalist startups for the MIT Clean Energy Prize, the nation’s oldest and largest clean energy business plan/pitch competition, Fall 2019 – Spring 2020

Next Sing: Founding and current act lead, current music advisor and workshops coordinator for low-commitment “act based” musical group based out of the Next House dormitory. My arrangements can be found here. Spring 2019 – Spring 2020

Other various involvements:

  • MIT Undergraduate Committee on Sustainability (Fall 2017 – Fall 2018)
  • MIT Undergraduate Energy Club: Climate Action Team (Fall 2017 – Spring 2018)
  • Centrifugues (Spring 2017)
  • MIT Happy Club (Fall 2016 – Spring 2017)
  • ESP (Fall 2016)
