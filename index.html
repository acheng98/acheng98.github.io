About

I’m Anthony Cheng, a sophomore at MIT double majoring in Materials Science and Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering, with a minor in Entrepreneurship.

I have a wide variety of interests and activities, but I’m currently passionate about solving humanity’s big problems in the environmental and urban sustainability verticals. I approach problems by combining data science and visualization with sustainable engineering in the mindset of an agile entrepreneur. I’m currently a co-director of MIT StartLabs and entry chair of MacGregor’s H Entry. I also enjoy reading, design, cooking, and other elements of the arts.

In the past, I’ve represented the United States at the National Geographic World Championships, performed at Carnegie Hall, presented at International Science Fairs, and been recognized with a number of awards.
